# CivicPulse Privacy Policy

CivicPulse helps Town of Babylon residents ask questions about public town records, draft civic messages, and share community feedback. You do not need an account to use CivicPulse.

The policy answers what data CivicPulse collect: CivicPulse receives the text you type so it can answer your question, draft a message, or summarize your Soapbox feedback. CivicPulse stores anonymous interaction logs, including broad document categories for questions, abstracted draft topics, and submitted Soapbox summaries. These logs are used to understand which civic topics residents ask about.

CivicPulse does not intentionally store names, phone numbers, email addresses, street addresses, Social Security numbers, or raw conversation threads. Before draft and Soapbox summaries are written to the local database, CivicPulse applies automated redaction patterns to remove common personal information.

CivicPulse may send your text to the configured AI provider to generate an answer, follow-up question, draft, revision, or summary. The `/soapbox/submit` step stores only the reviewed summary and topic and does not call an AI model.

CivicPulse is independent and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or associated with the Town of Babylon. Always verify important information with official town sources.
